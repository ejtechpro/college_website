Principles of Marketing

Description

Principles of Marketing course is a comprehensive academic study that introduces students to the fundamental concepts, strategies, and practices in the field of marketing. This course typically covers a diverse range of topics, including market research, consumer behavior, product development, pricing, promotion, and distribution channels. Students learn about the principles of creating, communicating, delivering, and exchanging value to fulfill the needs of both consumers and organizations.
